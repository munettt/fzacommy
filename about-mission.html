Mission & Vision

Faznur Az-Zahrah & Associates (FZA) was established on 2 July 2009 with a vision of providing its clients with the best and personalized legal services. Our mission is to be "One Stop Legal Centre for All Legal Needs"

At the heart of its operation, are determined and motivated individuals who place particularly strong emphasis on client satisfaction and employee development.

In rendering its services, the Firm applies below core values:

  • Professionalism
  • Integrity
  • Efficiency
  • Humanity

FZA aspire to become one of the nation's leading firms by having key geographical and transactional presence. As the years unfold, we progress closer towards this objective. Our vision will continue to fulfill our desire.

To lead requires innovation. New legal product offerings, client retention tools and strategies, employee evaluation and retention program, continuous legal education and succession planning are some of the areas we continuously seek to innovate.

We continue to envision success in everything we do and we define what is current and what is relevant, without compromising our core values.
